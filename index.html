he is now simply, just a Man...

before things get too bleak though...

What I'm moving away from...

  • Spending my nights away from family and friends.
  • Struggling to pay bills or even buy food.
  • Having to give crappy Christmas presents.

What I'm moving towards..

  1. Stability! -Something I haven't known since I left home.
  2. Regular hours that allow me to explore and take part in the events going on in this city.
  3. Not being so scared of the future.
I used to have a cool job....

I used to date this hot girl...

I used to sleep until noon everyday...

You drinking my Sake, Kemosabe?!

Utimately, One has to understand that desires change. The things we want in our life are allowed to go into flux and change our direction. Most things we experience in life are impossible to describe to others. Things like Heartbreak and Anxiety are untouchable, though they happen to everyone. Explaining yourself to those heartless assholes people who choose to let you go is unnecessary.

Change is hard, but you are never alone.
